1. Overview

This Policy explains what data the PetSpot app (“App”) processes, for what purposes, and how it is protected. We aim not to collect users’ personal data. If the functionality changes, this Policy will be updated.

2. Data we do not collect

  • We do not require sign‑up or sign‑in.
  • We do not ask for name, email, phone number, address or other identifiers.
  • We do not request access to location, camera, microphone, contacts, or files.
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs and do not collect advertising identifiers (AAID, etc.).
  • We do not profile users and do not sell data to third parties.

3. Third‑party services

  • Google Cloud Firestore (Firebase) is used as infrastructure to store and deliver non‑personal app content (e.g., public reference data). We do not send your personal data to Firestore. As with any network request, the provider may technically receive an IP address and other standard transport metadata.
  • Google Maps SDK is used to display maps. The App does not request location permission and does not store location. Google may process limited technical data required to serve map tiles under its own policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Legal bases

We do not process personal data. If future features require processing, legal bases will include performance of a contract, legitimate interests, and/or user consent (depending on the feature).

5. Retention

Since personal data is not collected, specific retention periods do not apply. Non‑personal service content in the infrastructure may be kept until updated or removed as part of App maintenance.

6. Security

We apply organizational and technical safeguards (TLS/HTTPS, access controls, monitoring). No method is 100% secure, but we maintain a reasonable level of protection.

7. Children

The App is not intended for children under 14. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
